General Rules

The Footy Picks is a score prediction game where players try to out-predict each other by predicting correct scores in various football matches. Points are provided for the accuracy of your prediction and each league has its own rules for who points are available. Players join leagues to compete with each other. Those leagues have games where each game usually consists of a selection of fixtures for players to predict the scoreline. Think of leagues like the Premier League and games as the group of weekend matches. Players can join any number of leagues with different rules and games. At some point in the future, players will be able to create and manage their own leagues.

The Leagues

Players can join different leagues which provide different scoring systems or join a league between friends. By default you will be added to the Footy Picks Official and monthly leagues and at some point in the future, you will be able to create your own leagues.

Winning

To win, it's simple. Score the most points.

Scoring

The standard Footy Picks scoring system offers points for correct score and correct result with double points on one joker fixture. In the future you will be able to create games with your own points system. Check out the Scoring sidebar of the league to find out what points are up for grabs. Points can be awarded for the following:

Correct Score: Correctly guess the scoreline
Correct Result: Correctly guess the winning team/draw
Correct Home Score: Correctly guess the home tea';s goals
Correct Away Score: Correctly guess the away team's goals
Joker Correct Score: Correctly guess the scoreline on your Joker
Joker Correct Result: Correctly guess the winning team/draw on your Joker
Joker Multiplier: Multiplier applied to your Joker score

Other Rules

Final scorelines are the final score of the fixture at full time, be that 90 minutes, or 120. Games that end in a penalty shootout or aggregate win following a draw in the match are considered draw results by Footy Picks. Predictions must be submitted before the match kicks off. Any late submissions will be scored zero points regardless of prediction accuracy. You may alter your picks at any point up until the match kicks off. After that, your prediction is locked in. If you have selected your Joker game on a locked match, you will not be able to switch it.
